Q: What is iHeal Digital?
A: iHeal is a technology-enabled lifestyle platform focused on daily habits, preventive living, community engagement, and non-clinical personal development. It is operated by iHeal digital private limited.

Q: What is the mission of iHeal?
A: iHeal's mission is to support lifestyle improvement and habit development through a moderated digital engagement platform, ensuring quality healthcare information reaches the community while providing a space for meaningful impact.

Q: Who is iHeal for?
A: The platform is built for healthcare professionals, teams, coaches, wellness businesses, and individuals looking to improve their lifestyle habits.
